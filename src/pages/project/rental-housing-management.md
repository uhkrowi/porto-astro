Rental flat, private and public housing in Indonesia for low economic citizen, supply and demand management of properties, facilitate citizen to book and rent house with ease.

Tech Stacks:

- Go
- Vue.js 3
- Quasar
- Tailwind CSS
- MySQL
- Heroku
